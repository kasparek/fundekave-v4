  Datetimes are specified as an ISO8601 datetime
  The ISO8601 format is:

    * YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss.sss
    * YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss.sssZ
    * YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss.sss+hh (or -hh)
All date/time values should be expressed using the <abbr> element, e.g.,
    * <abbr class="dtstart" title="2007-05-10T14:00:00">2 o'clock</abbr>

    * The duration property is specified as period (P) of time (see section 4.3.6 of RFC2445)
    * The duration format is (fill in ___ with digit(s)):
          o P___D (P15D means 15 days)
          o P___DT___H (P15DT10H means 15 days, 10 hours)
          o P___DT___H___M (P15DT10H20M means 15 days, 10 hours, 20 minutes)
          o P___DT___H___M___S (P15DT10H20M12S means 15 days, 10 hours, 20 minutes, 12 seconds)
          o P___W (P7W means 7 weeks)
          o PT___H (PT5H means 5 hours)
          o PT___H___M (PT5H3M means 5 hours, 3 minutes)
          o PT___H___M___S (PT5H3M59S means 5 hours, 3 minutes, 59 seconds)
    *  <abbr class="duration" title="PT1H">1 hour</abbr>
    * <abbr class="duration" title="PT15M">15 minutes</abbr>
